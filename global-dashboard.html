[01] Context Strategy

Project Overview

Environmental issues such as air pollution and deforestation continue to affect public health, biodiversity, and long-term sustainability. Governments and environmental organisations need reliable data to understand where pollution is worsening and where conservation efforts should be prioritised.

In this project, I analysed a global environmental dataset covering multiple countries between 2014 and 2023. Using Microsoft Excel, I explored trends in air quality, deforestation, and environmental indicators to identify patterns that could support environmental decision-making.

The final deliverable is an interactive Excel dashboard presenting key performance indicators, country comparisons, and long-term environmental trends.

[04] Analytical Verification

Granular Project Catalog Findings

FINDING 01

Myanmar recorded the highest average Air Quality Index (AQI).

Observed Data Architecture: Reached an average AQI profile level of 328.42, far superseding neighboring geographic index points.

➔ Strategic Business Insight: Persistent high AQI levels suggest long-term exposure to air pollution, which can negatively impact public health, economic productivity, and healthcare systems.
FINDING 02

Ethiopia recorded the highest average deforestation rate.

Observed Data Architecture: Logged an extreme average depletion value tracking at 4.52%, signaling constant resource infrastructure tension.

➔ Strategic Business Insight: High deforestation rates may contribute to biodiversity loss, reduced carbon storage, and increased environmental degradation, highlighting the need for stronger conservation policies.
FINDING 03

Average AQI remained consistently high throughout the ten-year period.

Observed Data Architecture: Air quality gradually worsened from 128.98 in 2014 to 136.99 in 2022, before showing a slight decrease in 2023.

➔ Strategic Business Insight: Although the decline in 2023 is encouraging, the long-term trend suggests that air pollution remains a persistent environmental challenge.
FINDING 04

Countries with higher deforestation rates generally also recorded higher AQI values.

Observed Data Architecture: Matrix distribution arrays indicate clear trend intersections where canopy loss directly connects with escalated atmospheric micro-particle indicators.

➔ Strategic Business Insight: While this analysis does not establish a direct causal relationship, it suggests that environmental degradation and air pollution may be linked, making both areas important priorities for future environmental policy and research.
